Website Copywriting

FAQ

Will outsourcing content affect the brand or vision of my company?

Not at all. Whether through a phone conversation or thorough research, I’m quickly able to understand my clients' vision and needs. From there, I adapt my writing to reflect the intended voice and tone.

What sort of things do you ghostwrite?

Books, blogs, bios...you name it, I can do it.

Will you come up with new content for my blog?

If you are having difficulty coming up with new content for your blog or newsletter, I’m happy to conceptualize ideas for your choosing.

Will you be able to upload written content to my blog or website?

I am familiar with Wordpress, Markdown, and Ghost. For a slightly higher fee, I will upload content onto your website. Alternatively, I provide content via Google Doc or Microsoft Word. From there, you or your web developer may upload the content at your own convenience.

What is your availability?

Depending on the scope of the project, I am available to write on a weekly, bi-weekly or monthly basis. Let me know what you need and we can figure out a schedule that will work for you.
