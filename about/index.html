Writer. Editor. Thoughtful Creative.

Writer. I’ve been a writer all my life. From writing stories as a child to majoring in English, the written word has always been of the utmost importance to me.

Editor. I love to edit. Since my first job as an editor during college, I have always enjoyed using my keen eye to create polished pieces.

Thoughtful Creative. I am naturally driven to help people through my work as a wordsmith and a thinker.

Regardless of the project, I strive to produce quality work that will engage the reader and leave a lasting impression.

Although I often focus on wellness, outdoor adventure, travel, and lifestyle, I’m always open to different topics and projects. I love to be challenged by writing from unique perspectives and researching new subject matters. I’m also extremely organized and I always meet deadlines.

When I’m not typing away on my computer, it’s likely I’m off enjoying the great outdoors. From weekends on the lake at camp (it’s a Maine thing) to skiing and mountain biking with my husband and pup, I use my free time to be in nature. I’m also a certified yoga teacher, an avid home cook, a grower of flowers and vegetables, and a bookworm (as every writer should be).

If you’re interested in learning more about the work I do, please contact me. I’d love to hear from you.
